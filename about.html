PoC is a research project that addresses the challenges presented to architectural education and practice by the Internet Era and its AI-powered applications. Its questions and unfoldings assist in educational projects of various architecture schools across Europe, including ETH Zurich, UdK/TU Berlin, University of Innsbruck and TU Vienna.

PoC is developed by DrSc. Jorge Orozco at the Chair for Digital Architectonics in ETH Zurich's Department of Architecture.
